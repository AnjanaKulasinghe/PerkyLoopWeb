Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PerkyLoop for Business

Do I need special hardware or equipment?

+

No! PerkyLoop works with any smartphone or tablet with a camera. Your staff can scan customer QR codes using the PerkyLoop business app. No expensive scanners or POS integration required to get started.

How do customers join my loyalty programme?

+

It's super easy! Display your unique QR code at your counter or on table tents. Customers scan it with the PerkyLoop app to instantly join your programme. They can also search for your business name in the app.

Can I customize my loyalty programme?

+

Absolutely! You can create "buy X get 1 free" stamp cards, points-based systems, or a combination of both. Customize your rewards, branding colors, logo, and even the messages customers see.

What analytics do I get access to?

+

You'll see total members, visit frequency, peak times, reward redemption rates, customer growth trends, and individual customer visit history. Higher tier plans include advanced analytics and exportable reports.

Is there a contract or commitment?

+

No contracts! All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. The free plan is truly free forever with no time limit or credit card required.

Can I have multiple staff members using the system?

+

Yes! The number of staff members depends on your plan: Starter allows up to 3 staff, Growth allows up to 10 staff, and Custom plans offer unlimited staff. You can set two permission levels: owner (full access) or staff member (limited access).

What kind of support do you offer?

+

All plans include email support from our dedicated team. Growth plan customers get priority support with faster response times. Custom plan customers get a dedicated account manager and priority phone support.

Can I use PerkyLoop for multiple locations?

+

Yes! Your loyalty programmes and promotions are shared across all your locations, regardless of geography. Location-based stamps (where customers collect stamps at specific locations) are coming in a future update.
