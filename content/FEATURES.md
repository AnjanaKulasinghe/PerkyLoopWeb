## Customer Features

### 1. Digital Loyalty Cards

**What it is:**
A digital wallet for all your loyalty programmes. Replace physical punch cards with a clean, organized mobile app.

**Key Benefits:**
- Never lose a loyalty card again
- All programmes in one place
- Instant access from your phone
- Environmentally friendly (no paper waste)
- Works offline for viewing your cards

**User Flow:**
1. Customer scans QR code at shop counter
2. Joins loyalty programme instantly
3. Shop card appears in their digital wallet
4. Progress shows automatically

**Visual Element:** Grid of loyalty cards with shop logos, progress bars, and "X stamps away from reward" messaging
