### FAQs (Business)

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No! PerkyLoop is completely free to set up. You only pay when you grow beyond 50 active members.

**Q: Do I need special equipment?**
A: Nope. Your team just needs a smartphone to scan customer QR codes. That's it.

**Q: Can I have multiple locations?**
A: Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans support multiple locations with centralised management.

**Q: What if I already have a loyalty programme?**
A: We can help you migrate! Contact our support team for assistance.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel whenever you like.

**Q: Is my customer data secure?**
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption and never sell customer data. You own your data.
